SLEEP ZONE's Fluffy Cooling Mattress Topper

Get the perfect blend of comfort and support with our Fluffy Cooling Mattress Topper. Designed for those who want a soft, cloud-like feel without sacrificing firmness, it's ideal for hot sleepers and anyone looking to enhance their sleep experience.

Softness Meets Support

Featuring 120gsm thick brushed microfiber, this topper offers plush, cloud-like softness that cradles your body. The 20oz 5D spiral fiberfill provides even, deep cushioning, delivering relief for your shoulders, back, and hips. It's the perfect alternative to traditional down bedding, offering the same comfort without the allergens.

Stay Cool All Night Long

Our advanced cooling technology keeps your mattress surface dry and cool, ensuring a more comfortable sleep environment. Whether you're in a dorm, apartment, or home, this topper adapts seamlessly to your space with its deep pocket design and flexible fit for 8-21 inch mattresses.

Safe & Hypoallergenic

With OEKO-TEX certification, you can rest easy knowing the materials are safe for sensitive skin. It's a great choice for anyone with allergies or skin conditions, offering a comfortable and clean sleep surface every night.

Easy Care & Long-Lasting

Designed for practicality, this machine-washable topper is simple to maintain. The elastic band ensures a secure and flexible fit, making installation effortless. Its durable construction and elegant gray color add a touch of sophistication to your bedroom decor.
