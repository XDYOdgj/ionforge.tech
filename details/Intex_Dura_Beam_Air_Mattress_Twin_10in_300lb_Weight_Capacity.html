Intex Dura-Beam Air Mattress

Experience the perfect balance of comfort and durability with the Intex Dura-Beam Air Mattress. Crafted with advanced Fiber-Tech Beam Construction, this mattress delivers a firm and supportive sleeping surface, ideal for both home and outdoor use.

Key Features

  • Fiber-Tech Beam Construction for enhanced comfort and support
  • 10-inch bed height for a better sleeping experience and added comfort
  • Sturdy design that can handle up to 300lbs of weight without sagging or losing shape
  • Velvety sleeping surface that is soft to the touch and easy to clean
  • 2-in-1 valve with an extra wide opening for quick inflation and deflation

Why Choose This Air Mattress?

Whether you need a reliable solution for overnight guests or a comfortable setup for your next camping adventure, this air mattress is a versatile and budget-friendly option. Its 300lb weight capacity ensures stability and support, while the velvety surface makes it ideal for guest rooms or outdoor gear.

The Dura-Beam air mattress is designed to stretch slightly during its first use, which is normal and will settle into a smooth, consistent shape after a few nights. This makes it especially suitable for camping air mattress applications or any temporary sleeping needs.

Perfect for Every Situation

Whether you're looking for an air mattress twin size for home use or need an air mattress that can keep up with the rigors of outdoor living, the Intex Dura-Beam Air Mattress is built to deliver excellent performance and comfort. Its lightweight and compact design make it easy to store and transport, while still providing outstanding support and a luxurious feel.
