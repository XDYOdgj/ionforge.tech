By keeping your knees properly aligned and separated, this pillow helps reduce strain on your hips, back, and joints, promoting better posture and overall comfort. Whether you place it under your lower back or between your legs, it adapts to your natural sleeping posture, ensuring a rejuvenating and supportive experience every time you rest.
