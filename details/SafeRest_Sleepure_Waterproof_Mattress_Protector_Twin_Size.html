SafeRest Sleepure Waterproof Mattress Protector Twin Size

Designed for comfort, safety, and durability, the SafeRest Sleepure Mattress Protector is the perfect addition to any bedroom. Crafted from a unique blend of 60% polyester and 40% bamboo-derived viscose, it combines softness with long-lasting strength. The breathable fabric helps regulate temperature, keeping your sleep cool and refreshing throughout the night.

With a deep-stretch skirt, this protector fits mattresses up to 18 inches in thickness, offering a snug fit that stays secure even during restless nights. The seamless design ensures it blends effortlessly with your bed, without compromising style or function. Unlike traditional covers, it features a zipper-free construction, making it simple to install and remove with no added hassle.

Key Features:

  • Waterproof barrier protects against spills and stains
  • Breathable viscose fabric derived from bamboo for cool comfort
  • Deep-stretch skirt fits mattresses up to 18 inches
  • Machine-washable design for easy maintenance
  • OEKO-TEX certified for safe and hypoallergenic materials

Whether you're using it at home, in a hotel, or in a dorm room, this twin-size protector is a versatile and practical solution. Its soft, stretchable pockets conform to the shape of your mattress, providing a snug and secure cover that stays in place. OEKO-TEX certification ensures that the materials are free from harmful substances, giving you peace of mind about the quality of your sleep.

Achieve a clean, comfortable, and worry-free sleep with the SafeRest Sleepure Mattress Protector.
