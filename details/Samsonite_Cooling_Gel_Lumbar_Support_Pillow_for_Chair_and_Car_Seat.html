Samsonite Cooling Gel Lumbar Support Pillow

Say goodbye to back discomfort with our ergonomic lumbar support pillow designed to provide all-day comfort and support. Perfect for long hours at the desk, on the road, or in a wheelchair, this pillow is a must-have for anyone looking to improve their posture and reduce strain on the lower back.

Engineered with a cooling gel core, this pillow keeps your spine cool and helps regulate body temperature, preventing heat buildup that can lead to discomfort. The high-grade memory foam ensures long-lasting support, adapting to your body's contours for optimal alignment. Whether you're sitting in an office chair, driving a long distance, or even using a wheelchair, this universal design fits most seating arrangements with a simple one-clip belt.   • Removable and washable cover for easy cleaning and maintenance
