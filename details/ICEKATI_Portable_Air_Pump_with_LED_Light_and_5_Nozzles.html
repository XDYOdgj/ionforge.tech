Introducing the ICEKATI Portable Air Pump

Whether you're hitting the trails, planning a family camping trip, or enjoying a weekend at the beach, the ICEKATI portable air pump is the reliable tool you need. This compact and lightweight device, sized at just 1.8*1.8*2.7 inches and weighing 140 grams, is designed to fit effortlessly in your backpack or pocket, making it perfect for on-the-go use. The built-in storage pouch keeps your air nozzle and USB cable secure and organized, so you're always ready to inflate your favorite gear.

Built for versatility, the ICEKATI air pump handles a wide range of inflatable products including pool floats, swimming rings, air mattresses, and sleeping pads. With a powerful 4kPa air pressure and a fast 200L/min wind speed, it makes inflating and deflating your gear quick and easy. This makes it an ideal all-season accessory for outdoor and indoor activities alike. The USB rechargeable battery, with a capacity of 1200 mAh, ensures you can power the pump with various charging methods, including wall adapters, car power outlets, and portable power banks.

To enhance usability in different scenarios, the ICEKATI air pump features a built-in LED light with three modes: dim, bright, and SOS flashing. This adds an extra layer of safety and convenience during low-light conditions or unexpected emergencies. With five different nozzle attachments included, it's ready for any inflatable need, from inflating your travel bed to setting up a comfortable sleeping pad for the night.

Key features:

  • Compact and lightweight design, fits easily in backpack or pocket
  • Powerful 4kPa air pressure and fast 200L/min wind speed for quick inflation/deflation
  • USB rechargeable with 1200 mAh battery, supports 4 charging methods
  • Includes 5 versatile nozzles for various inflatable products
  • Built-in LED light with 3 modes: dim, bright, and SOS flashing

Whether you're looking for a portable air pump, inflatable pump, usb rechargeable pump, or camping inflatable tool, the ICEKATI model delivers exceptional performance and value. It's an essential inflatable accessory for outdoor enthusiasts, travelers, and anyone who loves the convenience of inflatable gear.
